How It Works

Find healthy food options in your neighborhood with just a few clicks

Healthy Food Access makes it easy to discover corner stores and products that match your dietary needs:

  • 1

    Create an account

    Sign up with your email to save personalized dietary preferences and access healthy store recommendations faster.

    Create account
  • 2

    Set dietary preferences

    Tell us about allergies and dietary restrictions so the map only shows options that work for you.

  • 3

    Browse the map

    Explore nearby corner stores with healthy products highlighted and easy filtering by your needs.

    Browse the map
  • 4

    Filter by safety

    See which stores carry items that are safe for your dietary profile and avoid products with allergens.

  • 5

    View store details

    Open a store profile to review product lists, location details, and support programs like SNAP/WIC.

Our Mission

Healthy Food Access helps customers find healthy food options in their neighborhood. By connecting you with corner stores that carry nutritious products, we're helping build communities where everyone has access to healthy food that meets their dietary needs.
