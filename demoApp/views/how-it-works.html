How It Works

Find healthy food options in your neighborhood with just a few clicks

For Customers

Healthy Food Access makes it easy to discover corner stores and products that match your dietary needs:

  • 1. Create an account - Sign up with your email to save your preferences
  • 2. Set dietary preferences - Tell us about any allergies or dietary restrictions
  • 3. Browse the map - Explore corner stores near you with real-time filtering
  • 4. Filter by safety - See which stores have products safe for your dietary needs
  • 5. View store details - Check product lists, location, and support programs like SNAP/WIC

For Corner Stores

Get your store on the map and connect with health-conscious customers:

  • 1. Register your store - Create an account and pinpoint your location on the map
  • 2. Add your products - Build your catalog with items you carry
  • 3. Tag product details - Add allergen information and dietary labels
  • 4. Reach customers - Connect with shoppers actively looking for healthy options
  • 5. Manage SNAP/WIC - Highlight that you accept SNAP or are a WIC vendor

For Suppliers

Expand your distribution network and support food equity:

  • 1. Create a partnership account - Join the Healthy Food Access network
  • 2. Build your profile - Showcase your products and commitment to health
  • 3. Connect with stores - See which corner stores are actively seeking suppliers
  • 4. Manage relationships - Track distribution to partner stores
  • 5. Grow impact - Support underserved communities with better access to healthy food

Our Mission

Healthy Food Access connects customers, corner stores, and suppliers to improve food equity in underserved communities. By making it easier to find and stock healthy options, we're helping build neighborhoods where everyone has access to nutritious food.
