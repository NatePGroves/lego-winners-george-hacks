Managing Products

Keep your store catalog current and help customers find what they need

Why Keep Your Products Updated

Your product inventory is what helps customers find your store. By keeping your catalog current, you:

  • Increase visibility with customers searching for specific items
  • Help customers make better dietary choices with allergen information
  • Highlight what makes your store unique

Adding Products

Once you've created your store account, you can start adding products through your dashboard:

  • Step 1: Log into your store dashboard
  • Step 2: Click "Add Product" to create a new listing
  • Step 3: Add product name, category, and description
  • Step 4: Tag allergens (if any) so customers can filter safely
  • Step 5: Save and you're live on the map

Product Information

Here's what we ask for each product:

  • Product Name: The name customers will see when searching
  • Category: Produce, dairy, snacks, beverages, etc.
  • Allergens: Common allergens found in the product
  • Description: Optional details about the product

Keeping Information Fresh

You can edit or delete products anytime. We recommend reviewing your catalog monthly to ensure accuracy. Customers appreciate knowing exactly what's available at your store.

Need Help?

Your store dashboard includes a help guide and support resources. Most stores are able to manage their inventory in just a few minutes a week.
