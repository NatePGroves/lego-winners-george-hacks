Dietary Filtering

Find stores and products that match your dietary needs

Personalized Shopping Experience

CornerLink lets you filter stores and products based on your dietary preferences and allergies. Once you set your preferences, you'll see personalized results every time you use the map.

Supported Allergens & Restrictions

Our system supports filtering for common allergens and dietary restrictions:

  • Major Allergens: Peanuts, tree nuts, shellfish, fish, eggs, milk, soy, wheat, sesame
  • Other Allergens: Gluten, dairy, corn, mustard, celery, sulfites, kiwi, latex
  • Dietary Preferences: Vegetarian, vegan, kosher, halal (based on store tagging)

How Filtering Works

When you browse the map:

  • Green markers indicate stores with products safe for your dietary needs
  • Yellow markers indicate stores that may have products with your allergens
  • Product count shows how many items are safe for you at each store

Update Your Preferences Anytime

Your dietary preferences are stored in your profile. You can add, remove, or update them at any time by visiting your preferences page. Changes take effect immediately on the map.

Privacy & Data

Your dietary information is stored securely and only used to personalize your shopping experience. We never share this information with stores or third parties.
