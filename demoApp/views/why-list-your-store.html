Why List Your Store?

Join a network of corner stores connecting customers with healthy food options

Reach Health-Conscious Customers

Get visibility with customers who are actively searching for stores like yours. Attract foot traffic from shoppers specifically looking for the products you carry.

Key Benefits

  • Free Directory Listing - Get your store on the map with no cost
  • Product Catalog - Showcase your full range of healthy products
  • Loyalty Programs - Highlight SNAP Match or WIC acceptance
  • Customer Insights - See what customers are looking for
  • Easy Management - Update your products and store info anytime
  • Community Impact - Support food equity in your neighborhood

How It Helps Your Bottom Line

Studies show that corner stores with visible healthy options attract new customers and increase sales. By listing with Healthy Food Access:

  • You're seen by customers searching for products you already stock
  • You can highlight your competitive advantages (SNAP, WIC, fresh produce)
  • You contribute to community health while growing your business
