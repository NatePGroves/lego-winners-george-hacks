Partnership Overview

Join a network of suppliers committed to food equity and healthy communities

Our Vision

Healthy Food Access connects suppliers with corner stores across DC, expanding access to nutritious products in underserved communities. By joining our network, you're not just growing your business—you're supporting food equity.

Why Partner With Us

  • Direct Connections - Connect directly with corner stores actively seeking healthy products
  • Market Insights - See real demand from your target retailers
  • Growth Opportunity - Expand your distribution network without expensive broker fees
  • Mission-Driven - Support communities and build brand trust
  • Digital Platform - Manage all partnerships in one place
  • Fair Pricing - No middleman markups—work directly with retailers

Partnership Types

Direct Supply: Supply individual stores with your products

Wholesale Distribution: Partner with larger aggregators serving multiple stores

Consulting: Provide expertise on product sourcing or nutrition to retailers

How We Support You

  • A platform to showcase your products and company mission
  • Tools to manage orders, inventory, and relationships
  • Community and networking with other socially-conscious suppliers
  • Data on market trends and store needs

Get Started

Ready to grow your distribution network while supporting food equity? Create a supplier account and start connecting with stores today.
