Distribution Network

Understand how CornerLink connects suppliers to corner stores

Our Network

The CornerLink distribution network includes 500+ corner stores across DC and a growing community of suppliers committed to food equity. Our network is designed to be efficient, fair, and mission-driven.

How the Network Works

  • Store Inventory Visibility: See real-time product needs from participating corner stores
  • Direct Relationships: Build direct partnerships without intermediaries
  • Flexible Terms: Negotiate pricing and terms directly with retailers
  • Order Management: Track orders and deliveries through our platform
  • Payment Processing: Secure, transparent payment handling

Participating Suppliers

Our network includes:

  • Local farms and produce companies
  • Manufacturers of healthy packaged goods
  • Specialty and organic product distributors
  • Socially-conscious wholesale suppliers

Supporting DC Communities

By participating in our network, suppliers help expand access to nutritious food in underserved neighborhoods. Your products reach customers who might otherwise have limited access to healthy options.

Geographic Coverage

Our network spans across DC wards, with particularly strong presence in communities that have historically had limited access to healthy food options. Join us in building a more equitable food system.
